The DASH content imsc1_img_subs.mpd contains 2 tracks with IMSC1 image subtitles in ISOBMFF segments.
  • The images are sent as subsamples in the same sample as the corresponding TTML document.
  • The MIME type, codecs, and moov header should follow the CMAF DIS specification.
  • These files were generated specifically for testing the IMSC1 image subtitle implementation of dash.js.
